Dedicated to helping you achieve a meaningful change

Welcome to Synthesis Practice. We are a collaborative group of psychotherapists bringing together different therapeutic perspectives and cultural humility. We offer a safe space to explore life's challenges, deepen self-understanding and make meaningful changes.

Our team works across a range of evidence-based and depth-oriented approaches, including Psychodynamic Psychotherapy, Systemic Therapy, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, and Integrative Psychotherapy. This multimodal approach allows us to tailor therapy to your unique needs, experiences, and goals.

We believe that meaningful change happens within a relationship built on trust, understanding, and psychological safety. Whether you are navigating emotional difficulties, relational challenges, life transitions, or personal growth, we are here to support you with compassion, professionalism, and care.

Our Core Values

The foundational principles that guide every clinical interaction we offer.

Compassion
