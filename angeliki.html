Systemic Psychotherapist

Angeliki Tsiftsi

I am a Systemic Psychotherapist working with adults, couples and families experiencing emotional difficulties, relationship challenges and life transitions. My clinical experience spans NHS Adult Mental Health Services and Child and Adolescent Mental Health Services (CAMHS), where I have supported individuals, families and wider professional networks to navigate complex emotional and relational difficulties.

My approach is grounded in systemic psychotherapy, which recognises that our experiences, relationships and wider social contexts shape how we understand ourselves and the challenges we face. Rather than viewing difficulties in isolation, I work collaboratively with clients to explore the patterns, relationships and life experiences that may be contributing to distress, while identifying existing strengths and creating new possibilities for change.

My approach is integrating systemic therapy with evidence-based approaches, including Mentalization-Based Therapy (MBT), Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), and attachment-informed practice. Therapy offers a compassionate and reflective space where we can make sense of your experiences, deepen emotional understanding and develop practical ways of responding to life’s challenges.

Whether you are experiencing anxiety, low mood, family or relationship difficulties, or feeling stuck in recurring patterns, I aim to provide a safe, non-judgemental and collaborative environment where lasting and meaningful change can take place.
